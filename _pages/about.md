Linwei Tao is a first year Ph.D student at the University of Sydney, supervised by <a href="http://changxu.xyz/">Dr Chang Xu</a>. He received B.Eng degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2017. His research focuses on Uncertainty Estimation and Calibration, Neural Architecture Search(NAS). He also worked as a teaching assistant for COMP5329 Deep Learning and HTIN5005 Applied Healthcare Data Science in USYD.
